CQRj aboutus

Keeping the recent debates about the privacy and safety of consumer data in mind we have created an alternative for secure storage of data. People are unable to trust the foreign companies because of multiple allegations of data leakage. We have tried to solve this problem by building a secure platform to upload data and free up space on personal device.

Storing your personal data or any sophisticated data(Business related, client related etc) on a cloud system provides added protection from hackers and data loss. It is also important as in today’s generation “Data is the new oil” so we need high secure storage and Data protection. We have used two factor authentication using face recognition and the face ids are stored after encryption using AES encryption.
